get to know me

computers

I was born and raised in Reading, PA as the younger of my parents' two kids. I went to St. Alban's for preschool and Wilson for k-12, which is when I gained interest in computers.

In 8th grade I bought myself Diablo 3, but our family computer had no dedicated graphics card. So with the money I had I bought a nVidia GT440 and put it in the family computer. I finished my first machine in 10th grade and have been upgrading it since then.

My interest in computer science first began when I took "Computer Integrated Manufacturing" in high school, where we built and automated a small assembly line. I loved coding in the project, so I took a computer science course the next year and haven't looked back since.

photography

The summer after 11th grade I travelled to England, Switzerland, and France, so before the trip I bought my first point-and-shoot camera. The next summer I and two friends went on a two week trip from coast to coast, so to better capture I bought myself a Nikon D3300 and a telephoto lens.

Needless to say I am an amateur photographer at best, but I love to go to new and old places and capture whatever I can.

taskie

Taskie is a straight-forward task management app for Android. The project was created in Android Studio, and its purpose was to provide myself with an app that suited my needs as well as gaining practice with Android Development. Functionality includes adding tasks with a unique due date, organizing apps in chronological order by due date, a functioning calendar which highlights the days of each month on which the user has a task, and an independent shopping list. It is available on Google Play.
